Chiem-Seng Yaangh, Ph.D. Advisor/former Board President, Iu Mien Community Services, Sacramento, CA. An expert on Iu Mien culture, language and history.

Examples of Asian American groups has excluded Iu Mien (commonly known as Mien or Yao). Hmong is listed, but Iu Mien people. Both groups are from Laos. Laotian, historically, refers to all ethnic groups from Laos; but currently Laotian are usually refer to native Lao people. Iu Mien community through a group called Advocate for Iu Mien, https://www.facebook.com/AdvocatesforIuMien/, have been advocating for the inclusive of Iu Mien into the model curriculum. Please consider adding Iu Mien to the list of examples/samples of Asian American experience.
