Rebecca Buczinski

This document is very concerning to me. I feel that it is extremely polarizing. It clearly teaches a one sided opinion on history, ethnicity and culture. I feel that this will cause even more division among students and not grow compassion and unity among students in any way. It is damaging to suggest that a person needs to somehow compensate or make up for having a parent(s) that praise them, encourage them, or take them to a museum. This is not privilege that a child should feel that they need to apologize for.

I'm also concerned that there is absolutely no encouragement for a student to lobby their local politicians for conservative views. They are encouraged to lobby for liberal views, but moderate views are labeled as far right ideas. It's just a gross misrepresentation of reality. The entire document reads like a parody and even self proclaimed liberal journalists are appalled that this document would be taught in the public school.
