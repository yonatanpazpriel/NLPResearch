Please email ​SaveCAethnicstudies@gmail.com​ to add to our list, and/or to work further with us in 
this movement for one of the most critical educational justice causes of our time.  
 
We represent 50+ years of Ethnic Studies, as the primary communities,  constituencies, and 
stakeholders who brought this state-level project to possibility, through decades of struggle, blood, 
sweat, tears, intellectual labor, remembrance, knowledge production and envisioning of a more 
equitable education for present and future generations.  ​Save CA Ethnic Studies
